Solution: Juice Factory

Answer: WRINGINGALARM
Written by Ryan Liu

Each track of the playlist consists of two popular Apple ringtones/alarms mashed together. The names of the ringtones/alarms can be found online and are also clued by the images below the track.

The titles of the tracks each clues to an apple variety. The key observation to make is the fact that each of these apple varieties are in fact hybrids made up of 2 distinct apple breeds, and that each of the base ringtones correspond to an apple breed itself. The mappings between the apple parent breeds and the base ringtones can be deduced by comparing where the ringtones and apple breeds both appear. For example, JONATHAN is a parent of both AKANE and MELROSE, both of which contains the NIGHT OWL ringtone. It can be deduced that JONATHAN must correspond to NIGHT OWL.

Author’s Notes

This was one of our earlier puzzles written for this hunt, and one of my first puzzles I've ever written (I had written a few puzzles for a smaller audience, but never for a hunt of this scale). I tried to create a puzzle with properties that I personally value in ISIS (Identify, Sort, Index, Solve) puzzles:

  • An ID step that is not too annoying or time-consuming
    • People may disagree with me here here -- I apologize to all the people triggered by the alarms. ðŸ˜…
  • Thematic tightness (the title, every step, and answer should be related to apples/Apple)
  • A thematic aha that is not too difficult or obscure, while still being satisfying (apple breed hybrids)

Throughout the editing process, this puzzle did not see too many changes save for the presentation of the ringtone tracks and variations in the flavortext. I initially had too much hinting in the flavortext, then too little, and finally what we have now. We paid special attention to Android-using test-solvers to make sure that this puzzle was fair and solvable for non-iPhone users.

There was a similar puzzle from Inexact Hunt that made me think about pulling this puzzle, but we ultimately decided that it was different enough (e.g. usage of Apple ringtones, apple hybrids) to warrant keeping it.
