Juice Factory

You enter a juice factory and see that all the workers are fast asleep -- save for one kid, sporting a black turtleneck and airpods listening to a strange playlist full of hybrid tracks.

The kid asks: “I know laziness breeds innovation, but I don’t know how they all fell asleep. Can you help me wake them up? These pictures on the walls might help you.”
